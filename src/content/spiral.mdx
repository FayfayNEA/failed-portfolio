## The Challenge

## Project Overview

The designs of Schiaparelli are defined by a surreal, otherworldly energy, yet their runway environments often lack the same theatrical anticipation. The Spiral addresses this gap by gradually revealing each look through distorted lenses, creating suspense and fragmented perception. In the final moment, distortion falls away and the garment appears in its full, fortified beauty, aligning the scenography with the house's surreal vision.
