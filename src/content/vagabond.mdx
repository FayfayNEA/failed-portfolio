## Project Overview

Vagabond Radio is an interview series exploring the trajectories behind people's work and creative lives. Rooted in a longstanding interest in autobiography and personal narrative, the project examines how individuals arrive at their professions, what pivotal decisions shaped their paths, and how passion continues to inform their current pursuits. Through thoughtful, in-depth conversations, Vagabond Radio seeks to uncover the underlying motivations, turning points, and values that drive people forward. By documenting these stories, the project builds a living archive of lived experience, illuminating the diverse ways ambition, circumstance, and curiosity intersect to shape a career.
