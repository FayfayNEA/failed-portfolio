## Project Overview

A local band in Blacksburg approached us to design a limited-run T-shirt for their live shows. Sam developed the graphic identity, translating the band's energy into a bold, wearable visual. I led the production process, hand-printing approximately 100 shirts to ensure quality and character in each piece. The entire run sold out quickly at performances, demonstrating the strength of the design and its resonance with the band's audience. The project combined graphic development, hands-on fabrication, and direct-to-community distribution, resulting in a product that extended the band's presence beyond the stage.
