## Project Overview

### The Rundown

The Jahn Studio site functions as the digital identity and portfolio for the internationally recognized architecture practice Jahn, showcasing their global work and design philosophy. The site highlights major built and conceptual projects across regions and typologies, communicates the studio's legacy of integrated, innovative design, and presents its collaborative culture, leadership, and ideas in a clear, engaging format. It also serves as a hub for news, sustainability initiatives, and contact pathways, inviting visitors to explore the firm's impact on cities and communities worldwide.

## Research

### Target Users

John

58 year old John is a developer looking to hire a client to build their next apartments. They've loved Helmet Jahn's work since they moved to Chicago but wants to easily see what they have done with apartments in the past.

How might we showcase Helmut Jahn's work in a way that is true to its design?

By using a minimalist style with light levels of abstraction.

product display

Mobile Examples

The low-fidelity iteration of this project was developed efficiently, as interface conventions within AI platforms and video game systems are well established and supported by substantial research. I conducted comprehensive analysis of current large language models to inform the layout strategy, synthesizing industry best practices and identifying key strengths to integrate into the design.

conclusion

Considerations and Thoughts

Working within an established practice taught me that design constraints imposed by a client's legacy are not limitations; they are the brief. Every decision on the JAHN site had to answer to Helmut Jahn's existing visual language first. That discipline sharpened how I approach brand fidelity. For the next iteration, I would move away from WordPress entirely and deploy a custom build. The minimalist direction was right. The platform was the ceiling.
